Overview

Brand-led design with a conversion job.

Gaming web experiences carry two demands at once: they have to feel on-brand and exciting, and they have to perform — drive sign-ups, promote offers, bring players back. Working on Scopely's Classic Casino and Bingo Bash, I designed across landing pages, promotions and retention journeys with both in mind.

The craft here is energy with intent: bold, high-engagement visuals that still respect hierarchy and a clear path to the action — and that hold up responsively across the devices players actually arrive on.

The challenge

Energy that still converts.

High-engagement gaming design can easily tip into noise — everything shouting, nothing leading. The challenge was to keep the excitement and brand energy these titles need while holding a clear visual hierarchy and an unmistakable call to action, so the page converts instead of just dazzling.

And it all had to work across devices and campaigns — reusable enough to ship promotions quickly, consistent enough to stay on-brand every time.
